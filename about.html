About E-TRAX

E-TRAX is a smart expense tracker designed to help you manage your daily finances with ease and precision. Built with modern technology and user experience in mind, it's more than just an expense tracker—it's your personal finance assistant.

Start Tracking Now

Why Choose E-TRAX?

Smart Analytics

Visual charts and graphs that help you understand your spending patterns. Track expenses by category, date, and type with intuitive analytics.

Secure & Private

Your data is protected with Google Firebase authentication. We don't access your personal information—your privacy is our priority.

Mobile Friendly

Works perfectly on all devices. Access your expense tracker from your phone, tablet, or desktop with responsive design.

Edit History

Track every change made to your expenses. See who edited what and when, with complete edit history tracking.

Budget Tracking

Set monthly budgets and get real-time alerts. Track your spending against limits with visual progress indicators.

Real-time Sync

Your data syncs instantly across all devices. No manual backups needed—everything is automatically saved.

About AlphaPrime

Our Mission

At AlphaPrime, we believe in creating tools that simplify life. E-TRAX was born from our own need to track expenses efficiently without compromising on security or user experience.

Technology Stack

Built with modern web technologies including Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, and Firebase. We use Chart.js for analytics and Bootstrap for responsive design.
