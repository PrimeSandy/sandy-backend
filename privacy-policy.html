Privacy Policy

Your privacy and trust are extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

Effective Date: 2025
1

Information Collection

E-TRAX does not collect, view, or store personal user data on our own servers.

All authentication and data handling are securely managed through Google Firebase, using your own dedicated Firebase account. Any data entered by users (such as expenses or usage-related information) is:

  • Transmitted securely to Firebase
  • Accessible only by the respective user
  • Never viewed, accessed, or manually monitored by the admin or any third party
Your Data is Yours: We believe in privacy by design. Your expense data remains under your control at all times.
2

Use of Information

User data is used strictly for functional purposes, including:

  • User authentication via Google Login
  • Storing and displaying user expenses
  • Providing analytics and app features
  • Generating spending insights and budget tracking

We do not sell, rent, or misuse user data in any form. Your information is used exclusively to provide you with the expense tracking service you expect from E-TRAX.

3

Data Security

We rely on Firebase's industry‑standard security infrastructure, which includes:

  • Secure authentication with Google OAuth 2.0
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access controlled by user identity
  • Automatic security updates and monitoring

While no online service is 100% secure, we take all reasonable steps to ensure your data remains protected. Firebase maintains multiple compliance certifications including SOC 1, SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR compliance.

4

Third‑Party Services

E‑TRAX uses trusted third‑party services, including:

  • Google Firebase – Authentication & database services
  • Google Services – Login and infrastructure
  • Hosting Platforms – For application deployment

These services operate under their own privacy policies and comply with global security standards. We only use services that maintain high privacy standards and data protection practices.

5

Ads & Monetization (Future)

In the future, we may introduce:

  • Google AdSense advertisements (non-intrusive)
  • Optional paid features or subscriptions
  • Voluntary donations from users

These will always be user‑choice based and will never compromise data privacy. Any future monetization will be clearly communicated and will provide opt-out options where possible.

Notice: As of 2025, E-TRAX does not display any advertisements. We will notify users before implementing any changes to monetization.
6

Age Eligibility

E‑TRAX is suitable for all age groups. Users must have a valid Google account to sign in. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

7

Your Rights

You always retain full control over your data. Since data is stored in Firebase under your account, you can:

  • View all your stored expense data at any time
  • Edit or delete individual expense entries
  • Export your data for personal records
  • Remove access or delete your data entirely through Firebase
  • Request data deletion by contacting us directly

Data Portability: You can export your expense data in common formats for use in other applications.
